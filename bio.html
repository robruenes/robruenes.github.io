Personal Bio

My name is Robert Ruenes, and I'm a senior at Tufts University graduating with a degree in Computer Science.
I am currently searching for full-time employment in the New York City area.

I grew up on Long Island in the state of New York. In high school, I largely focused my studies in natural science courses.
I was an active member of my high school's Wind Ensemble and Jazz Ensemble, and was an athlete on the school's varsity fencing team; today, I'm the captain of the team here at Tufts.

Like many others I arrived at college expecting myself to graduate with a degree in biology or chemistry and then head to medical school.
However, after taking Remco Chang's Introduction to Computer Science course here at Tufts, I quickly jumped ships and haven't looked back since.

Since that class back in my freshman year, I've had a number of very enjoyable internships, working at MIT Lincoln Laboratory and Galatea Associates, LLC.
I am currently in my last semester at Tufts, and am taking courses in Web Programming, Operating Systems, and Concurrent Programming.
